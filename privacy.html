Privacy Policy

Lifebits: Learn Life Skills
Last updated: February 2026

Lifebits: Learn Life Skills ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you create a profile, we collect your name and learning preferences. This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Usage Data: We track your learning progress, completed lessons, quiz scores, streaks, and XP earned. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Provide daily recommendations
  • Send optional push notifications (with your permission)

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external servers or databases containing your personal information.

Third-Party Services

If you subscribe to Lifebits: Learn Life Skills Pro, payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Your Rights

You can reset all your progress data at any time through the Settings menu. Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the App Store listing.
