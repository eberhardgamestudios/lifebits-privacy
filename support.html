Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Profile > Settings and tap "Reset Progress". This will clear all your completed lessons, streaks, and XP.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your Lifebits: Learn Life Skills Pro subscription.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Profile, tap "Go Pro", and then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the subscription screen.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal data to any servers.

How do I use Listen Mode?

Listen Mode is available for Pro subscribers. Open any lesson and tap the speaker icon in the top toolbar to enable audio narration.
